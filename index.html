What is Bitcoin Dominance?

Dominance means authority and control. Market dominance is a measure of comparing the cryptocurrency market value to the total market cap. Bitcoin is the father of the cryptocurrency market, being a pioneer and having the largest market cap. Bitcoin dominance rate is 65.08%. Volume in crypto

What is volume in crypto?

The volume of a cryptocurrency shows the awareness of cryptocurrency and how often it is traded. The volume can be calculated for the last 24 hours, daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or even more. Investors can benefit when comparing the previous and the current volume of a cryptocurrency in order to determine the price of the evaluated coin, whether there is an opportunity for purchase or not..
